Info

I’m a Product Designer, Design Technologist and Digital Illustrator with 11+ years of experience in a variety of agency and enterprise industries including, e-learning, small business, health insurance, pharmaceutical and travel. In 2019 I relocated to Chaplin Connecticut and I'm living my best life, working 100% remotely and enjoying the peace and serenity of rural New England.

I consider myself passionate, creative, collaborative and strategic. I have strong ideas about good design and creating meaningful user experiences and I emjoy working with teams that values thoughtful and informed design methods. I get the most joy out of crafting and strategizing products that help make peoples lives better and easier. I'm a hands on kind of guy and like to be involved with everything from research, to design to front-end coding. Figma is my best friend and I'm getting knee deep in Vue.JS.

I have a variety of hobbies and interests outside of design ranging from video games and table top RPG's, to foreign language studies, to history, and most recently, herbalisim and homesteading. I believe strongly that having a wide breadth of interests informs my design and helps to provide fresh prospectives.
